Influencer Marketing and Management

Elevate Your Brand with Influence

In the dynamic landscape of digital marketing, influencer marketing stands out as a potent tool for brands to connect with their target audiences. At Ultimate Marketing Smash (UMS), we understand the impact of influencer marketing and management in building brand awareness, generating leads, and driving conversions.

Overview

Our experienced marketing professionals collaborate closely with your business to identify suitable influencers and develop a customized strategy aligned with your objectives. From influencer outreach and negotiation to contract management and content creation, we handle all aspects of influencer engagement for a seamless campaign execution.

Services Offered:

  • Influencer Identification:

    Thorough research to identify influencers aligned with your brand's values and objectives.

  • Campaign Planning and Strategy:

    Development of a comprehensive influencer marketing plan aligned with your overall marketing goals.

  • Influencer Outreach and Management:

    Proactive engagement, negotiation, and ongoing management of influencer relationships.

  • Content Creation:

    Collaborative content creation for compelling material that resonates with your audience.

  • Performance Tracking and Reporting:

    Continuous monitoring and evaluation, providing detailed reports demonstrating return on investment.

  • Compliance and Disclosure:

    Ensuring adherence to advertising regulations and transparent disclosure of sponsored content.

  • Reputation Management:

    Vigilant monitoring of influencers' online activities to safeguard your brand's reputation.

  • Crisis Management:

    Swift coordination with influencers to address issues and protect your brand's reputation in times of crisis.

  • Negotiation and Payment:

    Handling negotiation and payment processes, ensuring a seamless influencer marketing campaign.

Pricing

  • Influencer strategy and consultation services are available at an hourly rate, ranging from R500 (approx. $25) to R1,500 (approx. $75) per hour.

  • Our flat fee for influencer discovery and outreach services ranges from approximately R5,000 (approx. $250) to R10,000 (approx. $500) per campaign. Alternatively, we may charge a percentage of the overall campaign budget, typically ranging from 10% to 20%.
