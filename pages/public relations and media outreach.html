Overview

Our seasoned PR and media professionals collaborate closely with you to create a customized strategy aligning with your unique business goals. Through our dedicated efforts, we assist you in building a robust and positive reputation within your industry.

Services Offered:

  • Media Relations:

    Cultivating relationships with journalists and media outlets for valuable coverage.

  • Press Release Creation and Distribution:

    Crafting and disseminating press releases to announce significant events.

  • Crisis Management:

    Guiding and supporting in navigating and managing potential crises strategically.

  • Event Management:

    Assisting in planning and executing events to generate media coverage.

  • Influencer Outreach:

    Identifying and collaborating with relevant influencers to promote your brand.

  • Content Creation:

    Developing engaging content to position your brand as an industry thought leader.

  • Social Media Management:

    Curating compelling content and engaging with your audience for a strong online presence.

  • Reputation Management:

    Monitoring and managing your online reputation for a consistently positive brand portrayal.

  • Media Training:

    Offering effective communication skills training to your spokespeople.

Pricing

  • Hourly Rate: R500 to R3,500 per hour, with an average rate of approximately R1,500 per hour.

  • Monthly Flat Fees: Starting from R5,000 and going up to R50,000 or more, varying based on the scope of work.

  • Project-Based Pricing: Typically ranging from R10,000 to R100,000 or more, depending on the complexity and duration of the project.
